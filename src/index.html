Popular Causes

Public Awareness Program


Indigenous Rights Workshop

One of the major awareness programs conducted by NVDS is the Indigenous Right Workshop Program. This program was conducted in Kanchanrup municipality, Saptary and Lahan municipality, Siraha in 2012 A.D. The program aimed to create awareness about indigenous rights among the people of the region. The discussions held during the workshop helped the participants to understand their rights and take necessary steps to protect them.


Peace Building Workshop

Another important program conducted by NVDS is the Peace Building Workshop. This workshop was conducted in Rajbiraj and Ithari in 2013 A.D. The participants were local leaders, social workers, intellectuals, and teachers. The workshop aimed to promote peace and harmony in the society. The discussions held during the workshop helped the participants to understand the importance of peace and take necessary steps to promote it.

Reproductive Health and HIV AIDS Awareness Program

NVDS also conducted the Reproductive Health and HIV AIDS Awareness Program in Nawalparasi district in 2009 A.D. The program aimed to create awareness about reproductive health and HIV AIDS among the women of the rural community. The discussions held during the program helped the participants to understand the importance of reproductive health and HIV AIDS and take necessary steps to prevent it.

Become A Volunteer

Let's make a difference in the lives of others

Nepal Village Development Society (NVDS) is seeking volunteers to assist in sustainable development initiatives in rural communities in Nepal. Volunteers will work with staff and community members on education, healthcare, livelihoods, and renewable energy projects. This is an opportunity for individuals to gain new skills, make a positive impact, and connect with like-minded individuals in the mission to empower communities and build a better future.
